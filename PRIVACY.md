# Privacy Policy

**Application:** CSC Contract Hours Upload
**Operator:** UBC Department of Urological Sciences
**Contact:** chris.nguan@ubcurology.com
**Effective date:** 2026-05-23

---

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the **CSC Contract Hours Upload** web
application ("the App"), a Google Apps Script web application used internally
by the UBC Department of Urological Sciences to collect kidney-transplant
surgeon contract-hours documentation.

The App is provided exclusively for use by an approved roster of UBC-affiliated
surgeons and administrative staff. It is not offered to the general public.

## 2. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google to use the App, we collect and process the
following:

- **Your Google account email address**, obtained via the `openid` and
  `userinfo.email` OAuth scopes. We use this to verify that you are on the
  approved submitter roster.
- **Files you upload**, including their filenames, content, MIME types,
  and reported sizes.
- **Submission metadata**, including the date and time of each upload,
  the canonical filename assigned to the file, and the outcome of any
  automated validation checks performed on the file.

We do **not** collect:

- Your Google profile name, picture, or any non-email profile data.
- Information about other files in your Google Drive.
- Browser cookies beyond those Google sets for its own sign-in flow.
- Analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party telemetry.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used only to:

- Confirm that you are on the approved roster.
- Pin each submission to your verified identity, so that uploaded files
  cannot be misattributed.
- Record an audit-trail entry indicating which roster member submitted
  which file and when.

Files you upload are used only to:

- Store the submission in a designated Google Drive folder belonging to
  the Department.
- Forward the submission to the Department's contractually-designated
  recipients on a recurring monthly schedule.
- Run automated validation and analysis appropriate to the submission's
  category (for example, period validation on accounting workbooks).

## 4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

- Files and submission metadata are stored in Google Drive and Google
  Sheets resources owned by the Department's administrative Google
  account. They are not transferred to any other system.
- Access to those resources is restricted to designated Department
  administrators.
- The App runs as a Google Apps Script web application within Google's
  infrastructure. We do not operate independent servers or databases.
- We rely on Google's own security controls for storage and transport
  of your data while it is held in Google Drive, Google Sheets, and
  the Apps Script runtime.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third party for
advertising, analytics, or any commercial purpose.

We share submitted files only as follows:

- **With the Department's contractually-designated recipients**, as part
  of the recurring monthly submission cycle the App was built to
  support.
- **With Google**, only to the extent that Google necessarily processes
  the data as the operator of Google Drive, Google Sheets, Gmail, and
  Apps Script.
- **When required by law**, in response to lawful requests from public
  authorities.

## 6. Data Retention

Submitted files and their audit-trail entries are retained indefinitely
to satisfy the Department's contract record-keeping obligations.

If you wish to have a specific record deleted, contact us at
chris.nguan@ubcurology.com. We will honour deletion requests except
where retention is required by applicable law or by an active contract
obligation.

## 7. Your Choices

- You may decline to grant the App access to your Google account at the
  consent screen. If you do, you will not be able to use the App.
- You may revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time via
  https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.
- You may request a copy of any submission record we hold about you, or
  ask us to correct an inaccuracy in that record, by contacting
  chris.nguan@ubcurology.com.

## 8. Limited Use of Google User Data

The App's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to
any other app will adhere to the
[Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy),
including the Limited Use requirements.

In particular, the App:

- Uses Google user data (your email address and files you choose to
  upload) only to provide and improve the App's submission-handling
  features described above.
- Does not transfer Google user data to third parties except as
  strictly necessary to provide or improve those features, to comply
  with applicable law, or as part of a merger, acquisition, or sale of
  assets with appropriate notice to users.
- Does not use Google user data for serving advertisements.
- Does not allow humans to read Google user data unless we have your
  affirmative consent for specific files, doing so is necessary for
  security purposes (for example, investigating abuse), or doing so is
  required by applicable law.

## 9. Children

The App is not directed to and is not intended for use by anyone under
the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes
will be communicated to roster members at their registered Google
account email address before they take effect. The "Effective date" at
the top of this document indicates when the current version took effect.
